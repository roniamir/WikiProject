The Great Juba

Location: Ramat HaGolan - Odem Forest.
A prominent local feature and geological rarity. This maar, one of about 23 discovered in the Golan Heights region, was formed through an explosion of groundwater coming into contact with hot magma below the surface. The resulting crater is about 800 ft (250 m) across and 200 ft (60 m) deep.

The "Shanty" Shore

Location: Dead sea.
Special shore where sweet water meeting the solt water of dead sea. Park after crossing the first checkpoint near "Dragot Cliffs". Walk about 10 minutes east (the sea direction)

The "Merubaim"

Location: Megido high school. The observation deck is located about a hundred meters on the promenade combined with a monument. Continue in a westerly direction until you reach trees in the center of a field.
