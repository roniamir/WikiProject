The Great Juba

location: Ramat HaGolan - Odem Forest.
A prominent local feature and geological rarity. This maar, one of about 23 discovered in the Golan Heights region, was formed through an explosion of groundwater coming into contact with hot magma below the surface. The resulting crater is about 800 ft (250 m) across and 200 ft (60 m) deep.
